Master of Business Administration (MBA)

Duration : 2 Years ( 4 Semester )

Eligibility : Graduation in any dicipline

The Master of Business Administration programme empowers the students with Knowledge, Skill and Confidence to carve a niche in the business environment. All the key functions in today’s business organizations – Problem Solving, Decision Making, Strategy, Planning and Implementation, are incorporated in the course ans set in the context of the market place.

The MBA Course promotes the development of intellectual and personal skills within the framework of a rigorous business education, integrating academic study with vocational training, the course is designed to provide the students with a solid grounding in the core areas of business – Marketing, Finance and Human Resource.

We, at CIMAGE, put a great emphasis on a practical real time exposure to business environment through organized visits, Short Term Training, Projects and Discussion with Experts from manufacturing and service industries.

CIMAGE is associated with "Babasaheb Bhimrao Ambedkar Bihar University" popularly also known as B. R. Ambedkar Bihar University, is a state university located in the north prime of Bihar state in the city of Muzaffarpur in India. Established in 1960, this university has 37 constituent colleges. Distance education courses are also offered. Besides, the university organizes symposium, seminars and workshops. It is a premier institution of teaching and learning in the city and offers full-time and part-time courses ranging from undergraduate to postgraduate and research level.

The Courses offered by the University are approved from University Grants Commission (UGC). The university is a premier institution of teaching and learning in the city and offers various full-time and part-time courses ranging from undergraduate to postgraduate and research level. The university also acts as an affiliated link between several colleges and institutes located across the state in providing higher education. The University is a member of The Association of Indian Universities (AIU).

Tie-Up with IIM-C & IIT-D

CIMAGE offers the opportunity to get IIM-Calcutta & IIT-Delhi Certification courses for the students of Bihar, in addition to the MBA course being pursued by them. To do so, CIMAGE has signed an agreement with MacMillan India, IIM-Calcutta & IIT-Delhi.

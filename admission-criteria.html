MBA

Course Duration – Two Years (4 Semesters)

MBA(BR Ambedkar Bihar University) has a modular structure. There are four modules in this program. The first, second and fourth modules are common to all program participants. The third module is specialization module, where one has the option to select one specialization from four vital areas: Marketing, Finance, HR and Information Technology. In this program, the candidatures are highly trained with the foundation over basics and major paths of Management course. The specialization module includes Marketing management (MM), Financial management (FM), Human Resource Management (HR), Information Systems (IS) **, Operations Management (OM) **, International Business (IB) **.

ELIGIBILITY

a) Graduation (10+2+3) from a recognized University. MAT / CAT / XAT / ATMA / any other state level entrance test appeared.

b) Candidates with minimum 50% in graduation (under the 10+2+3 scheme) from a recognized university are exempted from the entrance test for the admission.

Admission in PGDM can be granted on the basis of MAT / CAT / XAT /ATMA / any other state level test. Interested candidates with below 50% in graduation are required to appear in the test to be eligible for the interview. There will be no cut off score.

c) Interested candidates if not appeared in any mentioned test and with less than 50% in graduation, if desirous for admission in the same session, are required to appear in the aptitude test to be conducted by AIMA/ Study Centers from time to time. They may be given provisional admission with an undertaking that they will appear in the aptitude test whenever finalized.

d) The OMR – Registration form, which comes with prospectus, is compulsory to purchase before the admission interview from CIMAGE admission office on payment of Rs. 500/- cash.


MCA ( BR Ambedkar Bihar University )

Eligibility : 10+2+3 or equivalent | Duration: 3 Years

MCA programme is meant for the students, who have completed their Graduation. The three year programme would be in keeping the various types of demands and expectations of the industry. On successful completion of the course, the candidate will be able to fit in well at higher professional levels in the industry and be qualified to expect top level jobs.


BBA / BBM

Eligibility : 10+2 or equivalent | Duration : 3 Years

The undergraduate Business Management Program of BBA/ BBM empowers the students with knowledge, Skills, and confidence to carve a niche in the business environment. All the key functions in today’s business organizations like problem solving, decision making, strategy planning and implementation- are incorporated in this course and set in the context of the market place.


BCA / B.Sc. IT

Bachelor of Computer Application / Bachelor of Computer Science - IT

The program had been designed for students who have just passed 10+2 and are looking forward to a challenging career in the corporate world. The program aims at providing exposure to both Information Technology and Management. After completion, candidate may either pursue higher studies or get into the field of industry and business.
